Customer Delight. The easy way.

Wootric is a modern customer delight tool for online businesses.

Wootric was founded to create a beautiful and actionable solution that makes Net Promoter program management easy. Wootric removes the manual and resource-intensive elements from Net Promoter implementation and tracking, freeing up your time to focus on your customers.

We come from diverse backgrounds. We have a common passion for the customer.

Founders

Deepa Subramanian

Deepa oversees product and ensures that her customers have the best tools to listen to their customers. She was most recently was the founder and manager of Schmendricks, a Brooklyn-style bagel boutique, purveying the best bagels the Bay Area has ever known. Jessica Pfeifer
